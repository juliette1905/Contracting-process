📘 Definition of Roles

Contract Requestor

The Contract Requestor is responsible for leading the overall Contracting Process, including:

  • Assessing Contract needed based on Transaction Risk-level.
  • Onboarding internal stakeholders, including Contract Drafter, where applicable.
  • Clearing the Contract Prerequisites during the Contract Determination Phase for non S2V-Supported Transactions.
  • Coordinating overall Contracting Process.
  • Securing internal and external inputs, organizing rounds of Contract reviews and drafting, and negotiation sessions.
  • Co-aligning negotiation strategy with internal stakeholders and the Transaction Owner.
  • Overseeing Contract Administration and filling in the Contract Repository (with S2V support for CLM-processed contracts).

Contract Drafter

The person who is responsible for drafting and negotiating the Contract.

Transaction Owner

The Transaction Owner:

  • Provides timely business and/or technical input.
  • Defines strategic contractual and negotiation options (in alignment with the Contract Requestor).
  • Prioritizes when multiple Transactions are ongoing.
  • Approves any final decision within negotiations, based on recommendations from internal stakeholders.
  • Oversees Transaction's implementation and evolution (including amendments, renewals or terminations).

Note: in the current CLM Tool, this function is denominated "Contract Owner".
